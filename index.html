About

Since February 2019, I am a PostDoctoral Researcher at Universidad Jaume I in Spain in the High Performance Computing & Architectures group.
I obtained my master degree in computer science at ENSEIRB-Matmeca in 2015. From October 2015 to December 2018, I was a PhD student at Inria Bordeaux - Sud-Ouest, in the HiePACS team, where I was co-advised by Mathieu Faverge and Pierre Ramet. I defended my PhD thesis on low-rank compression for sparse direct solvers in November 2018 at University of Bordeaux.

Research interests

  • Sparse direct solvers and low-rank compression
  • Graph ordering and partitioning
  • Multi-precision algorithms
  • Singular value decomposition and eigenvalue problems
